Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign up? ▼
Click "Get Started Free" on the homepage. You can sign up with Google OAuth for instant access or use your email and password. It only takes a minute!
Which platforms are supported? ▼
We currently support LeetCode and Codeforces for live stats. Support for CodeChef, GeeksforGeeks, and AtCoder is coming soon. You can manually add questions from any platform in the Question Tracker.
Is my profile public or private? ▼
By default, your profile is public and shareable via a unique URL. You can toggle it to private in your profile settings at any time.
How does the live stats refresh work? ▼
Simply click the "Refresh" button on your dashboard to fetch the latest stats from LeetCode and Codeforces in real time. The data is fetched through our secure backend and cached for performance.
Is CodeTracker free to use? ▼
Yes, CodeTracker is completely free! Create an account, track unlimited questions, link your profiles, and share your portfolio — all at no cost.
